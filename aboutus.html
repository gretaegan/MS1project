About Us

Based out of Westing Airfield on the outskirts of Dublin, our school is comprised of a terminal building, 4 hangars and our flight training facility. Our Boeing 737-800 simulator is located on-site in Hangar 3.
Our Instructors are all highly experienced former commercial and military pilots, so know exactly what it takes to succeed. Our fleet of aircraft are expertly maintained by our team of 8 engineers.
We look forward to welcoming you to our school soon!
